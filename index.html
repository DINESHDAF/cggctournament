Rules & Important Points.

Important Points

Umpire decision will be final and no arguments will be entertained with the umpire.

No profanity (abuse / slang) on the field.

Umpire in any of the above situation may call off the play considering win to the opponent team.

Extra 1 hour will not be added in any of the playing or non-playing member’s actual hours. He / she will have to manage his / her daily actual hours. Cggc committee cannot be held responsible for your actual hours.

Rules:

  1. In case, if a player is playing for two teams and if there is a match of both the teams at a time, then the player must play from his primary team and other team has to opt for substitute in his/her place.
  2. The revised substitute player selection will be considered FINAL till the end of the tournament, and in case if the player is available, substitute will not be allowed unless and until there is a valid reason.
  3. 6 Players each side.
  4. It will be played with Tennis Ball.
  5. 6 overs each side. Maximum over limit/bowler is 1 OVER. That means each player of a team must have to bowl max 1 over.
  6. All the runs (1, 2, 3 or 4) scored by the batsman will be considered only if the ball touches the boundary of specifically mentioned areas and no runs will be given if ball is stopped before the boundary by a fielder.
  7. Also after hitting the ball by batsman, if the ball hits the obstacles (directly or indirectly) like garden benches then runs of that areas will be given to the respective team. No running between the wicket.
  8. Bowlers need to bowl under arm. He/she has to do bowling without taking any run-up. He/she cannot go ahead as well as behind the bowling crease lines only during bowling a delivery.
  9. Ball has to pitch ahead of the center line (L2) otherwise it will be considered as NO BALL.
  10. NO BALLS and WIDE will not be counted as official ball and 1 run will be scored by the opponent team. If Batsman hits a NO BALL then specific area runs will be included plus one run of NO BALL. For eg, if any player hits a no ball to 4 runs area then his team must get (4+1= 5 runs).
  11. No wickets, no runs, no balls will be counted on dead ball (ball which comes towards batsman in rolling manner that too flat w.r.t. Ground).
  12. Full toss (ball thrown directly above knees level towards batsman) will be considered as NO BALL. Also if bowler’s any of the leg crosses or remains exactly on L3 line then it will be considered as NO BALL and advantage of 1 run will be gained by Opponent (Batting side) Team.
  13. If a Batsman comes out of the crease and played a full toss then that ball will not be considered as NO BALL.
  14. There will be no free hit for any NO BALL.
  15. Ball going directly outside the Box without interference by opponent team will be considered as OUT.
  16. Batsman will not be considered as OUT if he/she gets LBW. Leg-bye and byes runs are included as per the ball reaching that specific boundary area.
  17. Wicket keepers are allowed to change anytime.
  18. Stumpings are allowed in a match to take wickets.
  19. Please try to play ground-shots with discipline and also avoid any hooks or hard-hitting.
  20. Wicket will be given to caught out by directly catching the ball with either 2 hands or 1 hand. Also catching the ball after one bounce by one hand only, will be given as OUT by the umpire.
  21. In case of a tie match super over (of 1 over only) will be played. (Note: Not applicable for Round I)
  22. Captains, please be ready with your team 5-10 minutes prior to your scheduled match on the ground.
  23. If any of the player is not available during the scheduled match, then it is Team’s responsibility to make sure that someone from the non-playing members must be selected as per their choice (No Randomization here). However player replacement will be as follows:
    1. If a female player is not available then non-playing female member must only replace her.
    2. If a male player is not available then non-playing male member must only replace him.
  24. In case of a ball hit by batsman and gets unfortunately stopped due to any of the umpire, will be declared as dead ball.
